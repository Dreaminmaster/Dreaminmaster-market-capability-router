# Example: account appeal

User: 账号申诉了三次都失败，想在平台找人处理。

Expected direction:

- Friction: diagnosis + rule knowledge.
- Official: rules and final appeal decision.
- AI/market review: diagnose likely reason and review written materials.
- Self: provide truthful facts and submit the appeal.
- Block: password, verification code, full account access, backend manipulation, fake documents.
- Prefer diagnosis or material review over “guaranteed unban”.
