# Implementation Plan

## Objective

Build an installable, provider-independent Market Capability Router that proves the product mechanism before browser automation or large-scale data collection.

## Workstreams

1. Product and policy specification;
2. Domain models and local rule engine;
3. Seed knowledge and validation;
4. Skill and CLI;
5. Candidate evaluation and risk controls;
6. Evaluation harness;
7. LLM, search, vision, browser, and storage adapters;
8. Feedback-driven knowledge assets.

## Delivery principle

Each phase must produce a runnable and testable vertical slice. Do not build a large UI before routing, evidence, safety, and evaluation are stable.
